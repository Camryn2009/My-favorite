My Favorite Hobby
Playing Insturment

My hobby is playing an instrument I play the ukulele. In my free time I play the ukulele and make up songs for fun. I love the sound of the ukulele it's relaxing and calming. It's the main thing I do when i'm bored. The ukulele is a cool instrument and playing it is fun. On top of the joyful sounds it produces, it's also a practical instrument. It's portable, affordable and not overly difficult to play.
